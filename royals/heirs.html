About the prince
His Royal Highness is a Viking from the late era, although He is also known to be dressed up in all kinds of garb that He finds during His travels (both in time and location) that He finds comfortable. When it comes to travels He is known to visit all the places in the Kingdom and enjoying the different customs that exist. 		About the princess
Her Royal Highness is the first-born of a wealthy and noble family, that allowed Her to study and travel the world, indulging Her interests in learning about new cultures and languages. These interests brought Her from the isles of Insulae Draconis to the shores of Nordmark where She fell in love and is living happily ever after.
His Highness is interested in:
Brewing, fencing, heavy fighting, listening to songs and stories. 		Her Highness is interested in:
Fencing, dancing, archery, the scribal arts, discussing Icelandic (Old Norse) names.
His Highness likes:
Talking about and teaching brewing, fencing, heavy fighting. Listening to stories and telling stories. 		Her Highness likes:
To see the populace having fun. All kinds of dancing. Joyful fighting & fencing, especially where the populace can clearly hear/see what has happened in the bouts.
His Highness dislikes:
Modern mundane items visible at events, especially cans and bottles that look modern during feasts. Cameras visible during court, please do record and take pictures but keep the devices discreetly hidden. 		Her Highness dislikes:
Weak coffee. Mundane items visible at events. Being rushed or late.
Please feed His Highness:
Strong, Black coffee, smokey whisky, red wine, dark beer, white chocolate and meat. 		Please feed Her Highness:
Strong black coffee, smooth whisk(e)y, sweet drinks, cookies, milk choclate (Yes it contains lactose... Totally worth it.)
Please do not feed His Highness:
Sour beer and weak coffee. 		Please do not feed Her Highness:
Saffron, fresh coriander, dry prosecco, smokey whiskey, hoppy beer, anything rose flavored
His Highness has the following food restrictions:
None 		Her Highness has the following food restrictions:
Gluten intolerant and lactose intolerant. Not allergic but please make sure to inform HRH if the food contains either.
His Highness would like events to feature:
Time for socializing and relaxing, fencing and/or heavy fighting, beer and mead tasting. 		Her Highness would like events to feature:
Time for socializing, dancing.
